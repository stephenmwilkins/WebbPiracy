Webb Piracy

(Alternatively, Pirates of the CariWebbean)

Inspired by this article, which itself was inspired by this tweet, which itself was inspired by a meeting, you can now guide the Webb Telescope safely from the west coast of the US, through the Panama Canal, and to French Guiana, the home of the Guiana Space Centre.

Use your cursor keys to control Webb remembering to avoid those pesky pirates.

Developed by Stephen Wilkins based on this Pac-man clone.
